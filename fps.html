Unity First Person Shooter

This project is a first person shooter made in Unity.

The gameplay mechanics and features were made with C#. These include the AI's logic, player movement, gun mechanics, health systems and more.

There are two maps for the player to use and there are three difficulties: easy, medium, and hard. Each session is timed so the player can track how quickly they completed a level, their best scores are stored and displayed.

There are many other things that go in to creating a game of this scope. A variety of programs were used to model meshes, create animations, adjust audio files and edit textures. I have also imported (licence friendly) assets to help with this.
